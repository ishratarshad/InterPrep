##### Problem Identification
- What algorithmic pattern does this problem represent and why?
- Can you restate the problem? Mention inputs, outputs, and constraints.
- What approach will you use, and why is it optimal?

##### Complexity Analysis
- What is the time complexity? Explain.
- What is the space complexity? Explain.
- How do best, average, and worst cases affect your complexity?

##### Clarity of Explanation
- Can you walk me through your solution step-by-step?
- What data structures and algorithms are you using?
- Does your explanation address edge cases and testing?

##### Edge Case and Error Handling
- What edge cases did you consider?
- How do you handle potential errors or invalid inputs?
- What testing strategies would you use to check your solution?
